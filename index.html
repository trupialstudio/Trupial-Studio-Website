Grace Trupia

Founder & Creative Director
The Person Behind the Studio

Marketing with heart,
strategy with soul.

Grace grew up in New Jersey, took a detour through national service right out of high school, and came out the other side knowing exactly what she wanted to do. Her year with AmeriCorps NCCC took her across the country — rebuilding homes after Hurricane Harvey, supporting COVID vaccine sites in Texas, restoring community gardens in Missouri — and somewhere in between she started filming and editing videos about it. That's when marketing clicked.

She went on to study communications and digital marketing at Siena College, spent a semester abroad in Cork, Ireland, and graduated with honors in 2025. Along the way she interned at Hackensack Meridian Health, where she pitched stories that reached 147 million people, led a nonprofit marketing expansion for Girls on the Run, and designed content across social media, print, and web.

She recently packed up and moved to Seattle, where she lives with her two cats, experiments constantly in the kitchen, and is always planning the next trip. Trupial Studio is her way of doing work she loves, for brands she actually believes in.
