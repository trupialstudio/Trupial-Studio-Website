A name born from the venezuelan trupial — and a last name.

The Venezuelan trupial is a bold, brilliantly coloured bird known for its striking presence and fierce spirit. When Grace— whose surname is just one letter away — set out to build her studio, the name chose itself.

It's a play on her identity, a nod to her love of birds, and a declaration of purpose: show up vividly, make an impression, and always take flight.

The Person Behind the Studio

Marketing with heart,

Grace is a Communications & Digital Marketing graduate from Siena University with real-world experience turning ideas into campaigns that actually connect.

From pitching national news stories that reached 147+ million people at Hackensack Meridian Health, to leading nonprofit expansion marketing for Girls on the Run, Grace brings both creative flair and sharp strategic thinking to every project.

She's an AmeriCorps alum, a President's Volunteer Service Award recipient, and a genuine bird enthusiast — which might explain why she helps brands that take flight.
